Summary:
Fixed neo4j.ExecuteQuery (Tier 1) end-to-end support by solving the fundamental
incompatibility between the nil-embedded neo4j.Session approach from task-018 and

NewSession so neo4j.ExecuteQuery correctly calls our managed-transaction path.

emitting a bare OFFSET clause which SQLite rejects — now emits LIMIT -1 OFFSET n.

Added 25 integration tests in neo4j_integration_test.go covering all three tiers
and all v0.1 Cypher features (MATCH, WHERE, CREATE, SET, DETACH DELETE, single-hop,
multi-hop, multi-label, ORDER BY, LIMIT, SKIP, $params, node projection).

Key Decisions:
- Vendor shim approach chosen over go:linkname or driver fork: minimal, contained,
